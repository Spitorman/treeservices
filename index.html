SERVICES



Just below you will find an example of services we offer. please understand that this is an example only and we are capable and qualified to carry out many different types of work. If there is anything you need, do get in touch as we are sure we can offer services to meet your requirements.






Tree Managment


Here at MB Tree Services we undertake many tree management services, this ranges from Crown reducing, Crown lifting, deadwooding and everything in between. Get in touch to discuss your needs.



Tree Felling


If it's a woodland clearance or dismantling trees over a house, we have over 30 years of combined experience using a vast array of tools such as; lowering systems to heavy duty cranes to ensure the job is done correctly, efficiently and safely.



Ground Clearance


Looking to create space or simply get rid of unwanted brambles or bushes? Then there is no need to look further, MB Tree Services cater for all types of ground clearance. No matter what the job, we will take care of it for you.



Hedging Maintainence


Even with our major removal or management of trees, we also cover all types of hedge or bush maintenance. This could be a simple trimming and topping job, right through to shaping and developing existing features.

ABOUT US


We believe that the smallest things make the biggest difference



M B Tree Services is a partnership between Mike Brass and Michael boyd. Mike is a well established tree craftsmen with over 15 years experience. Since 2015 he has worked hard to build up a good reputable business which you all know today as MB Tree Services.

MB Tree Services work in a close professional manner, offering advice and tips on many tree related services. Mike and Michael both pride themselves on customer satisfaction and will only call a job complete when you say its complete.
